Is This For You?

You’re in the right place
if this sounds familiar

Cohortle works best with organisations that are ready to invest in doing their programmes properly. Here’s what our most successful partners have in common.

✅ We’re a great fit if…

  • ✓

    You’re running or planning a cohort-based learning programme

  • ✓

    You care deeply about whether learners actually grow — not just show up and participate

  • ✓

    Your programme runs for 4+ weeks with a defined group of learners

  • ✓

    You’re open to rethinking how your programme is structured and delivered

  • ✓

    You want real data on learning outcomes to share with funders or your leadership team

  • ✓

    You’re serious about running better cohorts — every single time

❌ We’re probably not the fit if…

  • ✕

    You need a self-paced online course platform or LMS

  • ✕

    You’re looking for a one-time workshop facilitator or event management tool

  • ✕

    You want a pre-built template with no customisation and no ongoing partnership

  • ✕

    You’re not yet ready to commit to genuinely improving how your programme runs

Not sure if you’re a great fit? Just reach out. We’ll tell you honestly.
