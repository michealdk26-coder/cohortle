About Cohortle

We exist to make
African learning programmes
actually work

Cohortle was built because too many great learning programmes across Africa were failing — not from lack of passion, but lack of infrastructure. We exist to change that.

Our Story

Born from a problem
we kept seeing

Across Africa, we kept seeing the same pattern — passionate organisations launching learning programmes with big ambitions, only to watch them fall apart within the first few weeks. Not because of bad intentions. Not because the learners weren't capable. But because the infrastructure to hold everything together simply wasn't there.

The tools didn't talk to each other. There was no consistent structure guiding the programme from one week to the next. Learners were dropping off silently. Facilitators were burning out. And nobody had the data to show what was actually working — or why things were going wrong.

So we built Cohortle. Not another learning platform. Not another app to add to the pile. A real operational partner — one that works alongside organisations to design, run, and sustain learning programmes that create genuine, lasting impact across the continent.

Our Values

What drives
everything we do

These aren’t words on a wall. They shape every partnership, every programme, and every decision we make.

🏗️

Structure Over Guesswork

Good learning doesn’t happen by accident. We bring intentional design to every programme we touch — so nothing is left to chance and every step moves learners forward.

🌱

Impact Over Activity

We don’t measure success by how many sessions ran or how many people showed up. We measure it by what learners can do differently after your programme ends.

🤝

Partnership Over Service

We don’t hand you a template and disappear. We stay in it with you — through delivery, through challenges, and through growth — as a real operational partner.

🌍

Built for Africa

Our approach is shaped by the realities of African learning contexts — not imported frameworks that don’t fit. We build for the continent we actually serve.
