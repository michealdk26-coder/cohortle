Why It Matters

Passion alone won't
sustain a programme

Across Africa, learning is thriving outside classrooms — through fellowships, bootcamps, digital skills programmes, and community clubs. These programmes carry real weight. But without the right infrastructure, most lose momentum long before they reach their goals.

Here's what holds most programmes back:

  • ✕ No clear structure or programme roadmap
  • ✕ Too many disconnected tools and platforms
  • ✕ Learners losing interest halfway through
  • ✕ No way to measure real learning outcomes
  • ✕ Quality drops when moving to the next cohort
