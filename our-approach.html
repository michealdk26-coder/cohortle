Phase 01
Discover & Design

Start with
deep understanding

Before we design anything, we listen. We spend time with your team, your learners, and your context — building a clear picture of where you are, what you need, and what success actually looks like for your organisation.

  • Stakeholder interviews and programme audit
  • Learner needs assessment and context mapping
  • Goal-setting workshop with your core team
  • Delivery of a full Programme Blueprint document

Phase 02
Build & Prepare

Build the engine
before you launch

Once we have a blueprint, we get to work building everything your programme needs to run smoothly. Curriculum, facilitation guides, communication systems, learner onboarding flows — all designed with your team, not just for them.

  • Curriculum and session design
  • Facilitator guides and resource packs
  • Platform configuration and tool integration
  • Learner onboarding and communication flows
Phase 03
Deliver & Support

Stay in the room
throughout delivery

Most support evaporates the moment a programme goes live. We do the opposite. We stay alongside your team during delivery — monitoring engagement, flagging drops before they become dropouts, and solving problems in real time.

  • Weekly operational check-ins with your team
  • Learner engagement monitoring and early intervention
  • Mid-programme review and real-time adjustments
  • Facilitator support and coaching throughout

Phase 04
Measure & Report

Prove your programme
creates real change

We build the tracking and reporting infrastructure you need to move beyond attendance numbers. Real learning evidence, funder-ready reports, and data that helps you continuously improve — not just record what happened.

  • Pre and post-programme learning assessments
  • Learner engagement and completion tracking
  • Qualitative outcome data collection
  • Funder-ready impact reports and dashboards
Phase 05
Sustain & Scale

Build something
that keeps growing

A great first cohort means nothing if the second one falls apart. We work with you after delivery to capture what worked, strengthen what didn’t, and build the infrastructure to run better cohorts — indefinitely.

  • Retrospective review and learning audit
  • Alumni community setup and activation
  • Next cohort planning and iterative improvements
  • Scaling strategy for growing reach or depth
